Hair Care Tips

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair gently.
  4. Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can cause damage.
  5. Protect your hair from excessive sun exposure.
  6. Enjoy your healthy, beautiful hair!
